Power & Colling

Our goal is to position ourselves as the leading provider of complete solutions for the backup and standby power generation market, offering a comprehensive range of products and services. In response to the current power crisis situation in Bangladesh, our Energy and Power team is dedicated to meeting the country's power needs by providing machinery and services for backup power generation and storage systems. As the sole distributor of "GENMAC GENERATORS" from Italy in the local market, we offer their portable/industrial range of gasoline and diesel generators. With over 28 years of experience, GENMAC has been helping customers meet their critical backup power needs by providing affordable generator solutions. Since its establishment in 1983, GENMAC has evolved from being a generator manufacturer into a full-service supplier, systems integrator, and power solution provider. Our services include installation, routine servicing, and electrical fault handling for generators ranging from 2 KVA to 2000KVA, powered by petrol, diesel, or gas. We also supply spare parts, consumables, and accessories to ensure the smooth operation of our customers' generators. In the near future, we plan to introduce a fully managed generator rental service that will include facilitating bank loans and providing refueling services. In addition to conventional diesel generators, we have recently introduced the world-renowned "LEGRAND" brand UPS (Uninterruptible Power Supply) systems. Our focus is on industrial-level UPS, offering both modular systems and conventional systems. We provide UPS solutions ranging from 1.25KVA to 120KVA, along with comprehensive support and services to ensure uninterrupted power supply for our customers. Furthermore, we are actively conducting market and product research to expand our offerings into other areas such as air compressors, construction equipment, rectifiers, IPS (Instant Power Supply), and solar PV systems. By diversifying our product portfolio, we aim to provide a comprehensive range of power and cooling products to cater to the evolving needs of our customers. Our commitment to customer satisfaction extends beyond the sale of products. We prioritize providing excellent after-sales services, including maintenance, repairs, and technical support. Our team of skilled technicians and engineers are equipped with the necessary knowledge and expertise to handle any issues that may arise with our products. At ARS, we understand the importance of reliable power supply for businesses and individuals alike. We strive to deliver high-quality products and services that meet the specific requirements of our customers. Our partnerships with renowned brands such as GENMAC and LEGRAND ensure that we offer the latest technologies and solutions in the power generation industry. In conclusion, our Energy and Power team at ARS is dedicated to providing comprehensive solutions for the backup and standby power generation market. With our range of products and services, including generators, UPS systems, and future expansions into other power-related areas, we aim to meet the power needs of Bangladesh and ensure uninterrupted power supply for our customers.
