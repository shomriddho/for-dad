Power Colling

We plan to place ourselves as the complete solution provider to the back-up and stand-by power generation market with a full range of products and services. Our goal is to address the power crisis situation in Bangladesh by offering machinery and services for back-up power generation and storage systems.

Currently, our Energy and Power team is dedicated to meeting the power needs of our customers. As the sole distributor of "GENMAC GENERATORS" from Italy in the local market of Bangladesh, we provide portable and industrial range gasoline and diesel generators. For over 28 years, GENMAC has been helping customers meet their critical backup power needs by offering affordable generator solutions. Established in 1983, Italian GENMAC power products have evolved from being a generator manufacturer into a full-service supplier, systems integrator, and power solution provider.

In addition to generator distribution, we also offer installation, routine servicing, and electrical fault handling for generators ranging from 2 KVA to 2000KVA, including petrol, diesel, and gas generators. We supply spare parts, consumables, and accessories to ensure our customers have everything they need for their power generation systems.

Furthermore, we are excited to announce that we will soon be launching a completely managed generator rental service. This service will include facilitating bank loans for our customers and providing refueling options, ensuring a hassle-free experience.

Expanding our product offerings, we have recently introduced the world-renowned "LEGRAND" brand UPS systems. Our focus is on industrial-level UPS, offering both modular systems and conventional systems. With UPS options ranging from 1.25KVA to 120 KVA, we provide the best support and services for uninterrupted power supply.

To further diversify our product range, we are currently conducting market and product research for air compressors, construction equipment, rectifiers, IPS systems, and solar PV systems. Our aim is to continually expand our offerings to meet the evolving needs of our customers.

In summary, our power and cooling product range includes UPS systems and PAC systems. We are committed to providing comprehensive solutions and excellent service to the back-up power generation market in Bangladesh.
