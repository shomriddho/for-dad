Operation Support System

An operational support system (OSS) plays a crucial role in the telecommunications industry, as it encompasses a set of programs designed to monitor, control, analyze, and manage telephone and computer networks. With the convergence of traditional voice telephone systems and packet-oriented Internet traffic, including Voice over IP (VoIP) and broadband applications like teleconferencing and DSL, the need for more sophisticated OSS systems has become evident. These systems facilitate activities such as ordering and tracking network components, managing IP addresses, monitoring usage and traffic patterns, and handling billing and reporting.

To ensure the smooth operation of OSS, ARS has established a certified and experienced resource pool. Our team is dedicated to maintaining high-quality operation and maintenance work for our valuable clients. We understand the criticality of OSS in managing and optimizing network performance, and we strive to provide reliable and efficient support.

Our Operation Systems Support & Performance Management products are designed to address the specific needs of our clients. These include:

1. Network Management System (NMS): Our NMS enables the centralized monitoring, configuration, and control of network devices. It provides real-time visibility into the network infrastructure, allowing for proactive management and troubleshooting. With our NMS, clients can efficiently manage their network resources and ensure optimal performance.

2. Network Performance Monitoring & Application Performance Management (NPM & APM): Our NPM and APM solutions allow for comprehensive monitoring and analysis of network and application performance. These tools provide insights into network latency, packet loss, and application response times, helping identify and resolve performance issues quickly. With our NPM and APM solutions, clients can enhance the end-user experience and ensure the efficient functioning of their network and applications.

3. WAN Optimization: Our WAN optimization solutions are designed to enhance the performance and efficiency of wide area networks (WANs). By leveraging techniques such as data compression, caching, and traffic shaping, our WAN optimization solutions help improve network throughput, reduce latency, and optimize bandwidth utilization. This results in enhanced application performance and a better user experience.

4. Helpdesk: Our helpdesk solution provides a centralized platform for managing and resolving customer issues and inquiries. With our helpdesk system, clients can efficiently track and manage support tickets, ensuring timely resolution and customer satisfaction. Our experienced support team is available to provide prompt assistance and technical expertise, ensuring smooth operations for our clients.

Our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction sets us apart in the industry, making us a trusted partner for all OSS-related requirements.
